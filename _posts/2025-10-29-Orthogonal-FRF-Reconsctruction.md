## Objective

The objective of this report is to compare Frequency Response Functions (FRFs) obtained from different measurement techniques and verify whether orthogonal and geometric projection reconstructions provide consistent results.


## Experimental Setup and Measurement Overview

Measurements were performed at several angular positions using both accelerometer and vibrometer setups.  
The following figures present representative FRF magnitude comparisons for selected measurement angles.
